Puzzle Project

For my Fall 2022 term at the University of Waterloo, I had a group project for my Communications in Biomedical Engineering (Visualization) course. We were tasked with creating a functional puzzle that incorporated animal and/or plant motifs of Canada. The details of this project will be attached in this page.

Our group started this project by creating various hand sketches and sharing ideas about how we could create this puzzle. After agreeing upon the final design and creating the sketch for the project, the puzzle was split up so that each member would equally work on each piece. The final design was concluded to be a maple leaf that is split into 5 parts and on top of each part would be a canadian animal. The canadian animals that were chosen were a moose, beaver, goose, and a polar bear. In the middle of the maple leaf, there would be the CN Tower.
