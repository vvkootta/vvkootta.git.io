Research Assistant

For my first year at University, I did a work-study referral to make money and gain skills on top of my academics. I am currently working with a physics professor and assisting them by doing tasks that they assign me. These tasks allow them to create their apparatus for the Rydberg atom interferometry experiment. These tasks exposed me to different areas of construction and design.

1. I designed a bracket that would be used to hold two rails together or move mirrors for the experiment.
I designed the bracket based on the dimensions of the rail. I did this by measuring the dimensions of the rail. Then, I created the design on SolidWorks and altered the bracket if any errors were found within the design.
